Elementname: Switch

Description

Create an if-then-else construct. The test attribute of each Case commands is evaluated until it yields true. The contents of the Case gets executed. If no test suceeds, the (optional) Otherwise gets executed.

Allowed attributes: (none)

Child elements: Case, Otherwise

Parent elements: AtPageCreation, AtPageShipout, Case, Contents, Element, ForAll, Li, Loop, Otherwise, Record, SavePages, SetVariable, Table, Td, Text, Textblock, Tr, U, URL, Until, While

Example

<Record element="data">
  <SetVariable variable="counter" select="3"/>
  <Switch>
    <Case test=" $counter &lt; 5">
      <SetVariable variable="text" select="'Less than 5'"/>
    </Case>
    <Case test=" $counter &lt; 20">
      <SetVariable variable="text" select="'Less than 20'"/>
    </Case>
    <Otherwise>
      <SetVariable variable="text" select="'Larger or equal to 20'"/>
    </Otherwise>
  </Switch>
  <PlaceObject column="1">
    <Textblock>
      <Paragraph><Value select="$text"/></Paragraph>
    </Textblock>
  </PlaceObject>
</Record>

Info

You have to be careful to encode the test with the rules of XML: that is »less« must be written as &lt; as < must not be part of text contents.

A Switch may be part of nearly all commands. It dissolves and only the contents of the Case or Ohterwise gets replaced by the whole construct.
